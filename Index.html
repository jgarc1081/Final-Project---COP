Roses

Roses have long been a symbol of deep emotion and timeless beauty. Often associated with love, admiration, and gratitude, each color carries its own meaning—red for passion, white for purity, pink for appreciation, and yellow for friendship. Beyond romance, roses also represent strength and elegance, making them a meaningful flower for both joyful celebrations and heartfelt moments.
